About Us

Our Story

Maripha Empowerment Foundation (MEF) is a registered NGO in Nigeria with a bold vision to advance food sovereignty and environmental sustainability across the country. Founded on the belief that sustainable agriculture is the cornerstone of thriving communities, we are dedicated to addressing the interconnected challenges of food insecurity, environmental degradation, and economic inequality. In partnership with Passion of Hope International, we champion empowerment, self-reliance, and the transformative potential of regenerative agriculture.


At the heart of our work is our 15-acre farm in Ilorin, Kwara State, a dynamic hub where we demonstrate the power of regenerative and integrated farming practices as the pathway to food sovereignty. This farm is more than just a production site—it is a living classroom, a research facility, and a beacon of innovation. Here, the next generation are inspired to see farming as a harmonious “dance with nature,” while local farmers are equipped with the tools and knowledge to become pioneers of sustainable agriculture. Through hands-on training, workshops, and collaborative projects, we are redefining what it means to farm in harmony with the environment.


As a Pan-African organization, MEF is deeply rooted in the belief that Africa has the potential to not only feed itself, but also to lead the world in sustainable food production. We are committed to preserving and integrating Indigenous knowledge with modern agricultural practices, ensuring that traditional wisdom informs innovative solutions. Through initiatives like Nollywood Eatz, we celebrate Africa’s rich culinary heritage by promoting excellence in farm-to-fork cuisine, bridging the gap between agriculture, culture, and community.


At MEF, we envision a future where Africa’s communities thrive in harmony with the environment, where food sovereignty is a reality, and where every individual has the opportunity to contribute to a regenerative and equitable food system. Together, we are sowing the seeds of change, cultivating resilience, and building a legacy of sustainability and shared prosperity for generations to come.


Our story is one of resilience, innovation, and a deep commitment to empowering women, youth, and rural communities. We recognize that true transformation requires more than just access to resources—it requires mentorship, education, and financial inclusion. That’s why we are dedicated to mentoring the next generation of agro-entrepreneurs, providing them with the skills, confidence, and support they need to succeed. By fostering equitable access to resources and opportunities, we are breaking down barriers and creating pathways for sustainable livelihoods.
