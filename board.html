Our Board

Meet Our Board

  • MARIAM OLORUNDARE

    President

    Mariam Titilope Olorundare is an entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in agriculture, food, and development. She founded Nollywood Eatz by Maripha and champions food sovereignty and community empowerment.

  • PAMELA C. ETO

    Board Member

    Pamela C. Eto is a Project Coordinator specializing in Software Implementation in Toronto. With advanced degrees in Petroleum Engineering and Project Management, she is passionate about agriculture and innovation.

  • VICTOR IBETO

    Board Member

    Victor Ibeto is a lawyer and advocate for sustainable development, focusing on agriculture and renewable energy. He emphasizes a holistic approach to environmental stewardship and inclusive growth.

  • OLUBUNMI A. OMOTESHO

    Board Member

    Professor Olubunmi A. Omotesho is a leading academic in Agricultural Economics at the University of Ilorin. With decades of experience and numerous leadership roles, he now serves as Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Academic).

  • BRIAN STEPHENSON

    Board Member

    Brian Stephenson is a founder and President with over 40 years in engineering consulting and development initiatives. He is an advocate for sustainable practices and regenerative agriculture across Africa.

  • OLUSOLA SERIKI

    Board Member

    Olusola Seriki is the CEO of MVUSA Projekts and a veteran real estate developer. He integrates regional economic planning with real estate development and champions innovative public-private partnerships.
