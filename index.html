Welcome to Maripha Empowerment Foundation

Our mission is to advance food sovereignty, environmental sustainability, and community empowerment through regenerative agriculture, innovation, and the preservation of Indigenous knowledge. We are dedicated to empowering women and youth, fostering rural economic development, and mentoring the next generation of agro-entrepreneurs. By promoting financial inclusion and equitable resource access, we strive to build resilient, thriving communities for a sustainable future.

Maripha Empowerment Fondation Partners with UNILORIN

The Maripha Empowerment Foundation and the University of Ilorin (UNILORIN) have partnered to drive community development and empowerment through education and skill-building. Combining UNILORIN’s academic expertise with Maripha’s social impact focus, they address poverty, youth empowerment, and sustainability. Initiatives include workshops, mentorship, and outreach programs, aiming to transform lives and foster inclusive growth. This collaboration sets a benchmark for impactful university-community partnerships.

Maripha Farms School Tour - Empowering the Next Generation

Maripha Farms School Tour is an inspiring initiative designed to empower the next generation through hands-on agricultural education. By immersing students in sustainable farming practices, the program fosters environmental awareness, innovation, and a deeper connection to nature. Through interactive tours, students gain valuable insights into agroecology, food systems, and the importance of sustainability, equipping them to become future leaders in creating a greener, more resilient world.
